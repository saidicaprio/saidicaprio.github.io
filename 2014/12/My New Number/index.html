My New Number

Today I went to Tsinghua University and picked a new phone number: 156-0090-3760 which is a 3G number managed by China Unicom. It cost me ¥180.

2:50pm leave for there, 3:50pm I arrive at Souhu Internet Building, and other companies like Google China, It’s grandiose, when I saw that building I feel that I can work hard to get there.

Then I walk into the collage,the other highest institution of higher education in China, I admire them who was admitted by Tsinghua. If I study hard 10 times maybe I can be admitted too.

Honestly, when I get into the campus I feel that it’s a normal collage, looks same as my Alma Mater, nothing particular except higher knowledge level students. I walk along the road feeling the piercing cold wind.
Then I then left to Water-Leaf Tsinghua, It’s a beautiful park. My iPhone’s battery is bad, always auto-shut down.Then I lost the way and only to seek the position by the cross map. Finally, I found a campus market street. Then got a new number.

When I get out form the gate, It’s almost 17:30pm, the sky slowly became dark, then I made a mistake: wrong direction.
I was lucky, found the bus station soon.
The traffic is really bad in Beijing, I hate that.
